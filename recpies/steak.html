Steak Recipe

Description:

Who doesn't like a perfectly cooked steak!? A pefectly cooked, medium-rare steak is sure to bring out the cave-man in anyone. Basted in butter, garlic and thyme, you can't beat the classic taste and texture of a well-built crust. Follow this recipe to bring out the carnivore in you!

Instructions:

  1. Heat a cast-iron pan on med-high heat
  2. Allow steak to temper to room-temperature, then season with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
  3. Once pan is smoking, put a generous amount of olive oil, or ghee in the pan
  4. Sear steak for 5 minutes each side (depending on thickness)
  5. Place a generous knob of butter in the pan along with garlic, thyme, and any other preferred aromatics.
  6. Baste the steak for an additional 2 minutes each side
  7. Remove the steak and allow it to rest for 10 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
