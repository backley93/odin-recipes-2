Baked Salmon Recipe

Description:

This is a recipe for baked salmon. Follow this guide and you can create the most delicate, flaky and fragrant baked salmon on Earth! Impress friends, a date, or your family with the culinary mastery gained through this recipe!

Instructions:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375 F
  2. Rub Salmon with olive oil and salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  3. Place Salmon skin-side down on a baking sheet, and put it in the oven
  4. Bake Salmon for 20 minutes, or until desired doneness is reached
  5. Allow 5 minutes to rest the Salmon, then serve!
