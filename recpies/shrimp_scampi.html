Shrimp Scampi Recipe

Description:

Shrimp scampi combines wonderful al-dente pasta, with the great flavors of butter, garlic, lemon, pepper flakes and white wine. Of course, we can't forget the star of the dish, the SHRIMP! Cooked to perfection, this dish is sure to impress anyone priveleged enough to get a taste.

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil (don't forget the salt)
  2. Place 16 oz. of linguine pasta in the boiling water
  3. Cook pasta for 8 minutes, then strain, saving 1 cup of pasta water
  4. Heat a large pan over med-high heat
  5. Toss shrimp in olive oil, salt and pepper
  6. Cook shrimp 2 minutes each side, then set aside
  7. Put garlic, and 1/4 cup of butter in the same pan
  8. Cook garlic until fragrant, then deglaze pan with 1 cup wine
  9. Reduce wine for 5 minutes then put shrimp back in with pasta, water lemon juice and red pepper flake
  10. Cook until water is just reduced down, then serve!
